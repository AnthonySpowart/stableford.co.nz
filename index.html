Welcome to Stableford a new comfortable home with mountain views situated next door to the prestigious Russley Golf Club. We are the closest B&B to Christchurch Airport with a bus stop to the city nearby. There is a separate guest lounge and tea and coffee making facilities are available. We have a reasonable tariff and we pride ourselves on our clean, quiet and friendly home.

Please feel free to look around our site and contact us if you have any enquiries. Thank you.

Our B & B has not been affected by the earthquake. We are up and running with all facilities available.
